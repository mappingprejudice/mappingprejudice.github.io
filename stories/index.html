Stories

Mapping racial covenants in just the beginning. These stories explore the human side of structural racism in Minneapolis, and how covenants shaped the lives of the cities residents.

Response to Minneapolis 2040

The Mapping Prejudice Team

August 30, 2018

Dueling lawn signs illuminate the passions sparked by the 2040 plan proposed by the Community Planning and Economic Development department of the city of Minneapolis. This debate gives Minneapolitans the opportunity to think about how they want their values to be reflected in the urban landscape of their city.

The Stories of Bad Deeds

Kirsten Delegard

July 6, 2018

We want this space to be the place where we tell the stories of bad deeds. We have all witnessed bad deeds. But this blog will be focused on the bad deeds in Hennepin County, specifically the property deeds that were saddled with racial restrictions. These deeds made it illegal for people who were not white to live in or occupy the property attached to these documents.
